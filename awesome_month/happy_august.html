Happy August

Interesting Facts About August

  • August was originally named Sextilis in Latin, as it was the sixth month in the early Roman calendar.

  • The Roman Emperor Augustus defeated Cleopatra and Mark Antony in August..

  • This victory helped solidify his power and the month was later named in his honor. Augustus Caesar.

  • August has 31 days because Augustus wanted his month to be as long as Julius Caesar’s (July).

  • The first British Empire Games (now Commonwealth Games) were held in August 1930.

  • India and Pakistan both gained independence in August 1947.

  • Elvis Presley died in August — on August 16, 1977, an event mourned by millions worldwide.

  • Perseids meteor shower peaks in mid-August every year.

  • August 24, 79 AD Mount Vesuvius erupted, burying Pompeii and Herculaneum town, one of the most famous volcanic disasters in history.

  • In the Southern Hemisphere, August is the equivalent of February.
